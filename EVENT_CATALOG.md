# Event catalog

Identity events are `organization.created`, `auth.login`, `auth.logout`, and `user.created`. Asset audit events are `asset.created`, `asset.updated`, `asset.deleted`, `asset_area.created`, `asset_area.updated`, `asset_area.deleted`, `equipment.created`, `equipment.updated` and `equipment.deleted`. The authenticated organization-scoped SSE stream currently publishes `activity.created`, `activity.updated`, `activity.deleted`, `vessel.position_updated`, `weather.observed`, `weather.forecast_updated`, `alert.created` and `operational_window.created`. They carry an ID, organization ID, type, occurrence time and payload; delivery is process-local in this local-first MVP and is not durable or replayable.
